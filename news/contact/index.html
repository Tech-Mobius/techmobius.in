Ask A Question

I’ve seen people wearing Tech Mobius hoodies and shirts. Does the club have its own merch?

Yes! Each year, our core members receive exclusive Tech Mobius merchandise — hoodies, tees, and jackets designed to represent the club’s identity and spirit of innovation. These are exclusively for official members and are not available for public purchase.

What am I getting into if I join Tech Mobius?

Joining Tech Mobius means stepping into a collaborative, high-energy community of tech enthusiasts, creators, and innovators. You’ll get the chance to participate in inter-school technology symposiums, hackathons, and competitions across the circuit — opportunities typically open only to official club members. It’s the perfect platform to grow, compete, and make a mark.

Does Tech Mobius teach any technical skills?

Not in a formal classroom way. Instead, we believe in learning through collaboration and creation. Our members include specialists in every major domain — programming, design, AI, cybersecurity, robotics, and more — who are always willing to guide and mentor you. You’ll gain practical experience through projects, competitions, and real-world problem-solving.

What are the criteria for joining Tech Mobius?

There are two ways to become a member: Earn recognition in major inter-school technology competitions or symposiums hosted by other institutions. Showcase your skills directly to us — if you have a strong portfolio, project, or achievement that demonstrates your capabilities, we’ll be glad to evaluate it. (Note: Competing in events doesn’t automatically guarantee membership — it’s your skill, dedication, and consistency that matter most.)

Can alumni or past members still contribute?

Absolutely. Tech Mobius has always been built on mentorship and legacy. Our alumni — many of whom are now excelling in universities or the tech industry — frequently return to mentor members, conduct workshops, and help organize major events.
